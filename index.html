ARUNIMA SINHA


About the Legend

  • She is the world's first female amputee to scale Mount Everest(Asia)


  • Arunima Sinha was born in Ambedkar Nagar near Lucknow in Uttar Pradesh. Her father was in the Indian Army and her mother was a supervisor in the health department.


  • She had an elder sister and a younger brother.After her father died, her mother tried to take care of her family Arunima liked football and also was a national volleyball player.


  • She wanted to join the paramilitary forces. She got a call letter from the CISF and faced her life-changing accident while travelling to Delhi. Robbers snatched her bag and pushed her out of the running train. She fell on the track and was unable to move due to her severe injuries. A train coming from the opposite side ran over her leg below the knee. She was rushed to the hospital with serious leg and pelvic injuries and lost her leg after doctors amputated it to save her life.


  • She was then inspired by cricketer Yuvraj Singh and decided to climb the Mount Everest after she took training from Nehru Institute of Mountaineering.


  • She climbed Mount Everest with a prosthetic leg.


  • Arunima Sinha was the first women to climb the Mount Everest with a prosthetic leg.she stood as an inspiration to may people those who think who can't achieve anything in their life.


  • She represnting all the women to be strong and bold even if the world thinks that she can't do anything.


  • She then wrote book in the year 2014 which was named as- "Born Again on The Mountain".
